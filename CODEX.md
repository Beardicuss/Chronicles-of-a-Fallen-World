## ‚ò£Ô∏è The Blight & Corruption System

The core mechanic represents Dagon's struggle with his inherited power. Using **Blight Magic** increases **Corruption Saturation**.

### The Stacking Curse
Each time saturation reaches 100%, +1 **Curse Level** is gained. These effects are cumulative:

1.  **Fading Vitality**: Max HP reduced by 5-10%.
2.  **Siphon Weakness**: Max Blight Pool reduced by 5-10%.
3.  **Cracked Shell**: Defense/Armor Rating reduced by 5-10%.
4.  **Draining Fury**: Weapon Attack Damage reduced by 5-10%.
5.  **Echoing Madness**: Periodic chance to be staggered or interrupted.
6.  **Total Corruption**: All debuffs begin scaling further.

### Purification
Dagon must find **Altars of Equilibrium** and offer an **Echo of Equilibrium** (rare drops from powerful Blighted entities) to reset all Curse Levels.
